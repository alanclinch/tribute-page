Nelson Mandela

Some moments from his Life

  • 18 Jul 1918 - Born in Mvezo, South Africa
  • 12 Jun 1964 - Sentenced to life imprisonment for fighting against apartheid
  • 11 Feb 1990 - Leaves prison in front of massive crowds
  • 10 May 1994 - Becomes the first black president of South Africa
  • 05 Dec 2003 - Dies from a respiratory infection
    • "I have fought against white domination and I have fought against black domination. I have carried the ideal of a democratic and free society in which all persons live together in harmony and with equal opportunity. It is an ideal which I hope to live for and to achieve. But, if needs be, it is an ideal for which I am prepared to die." Nelson Mandela, 1964
